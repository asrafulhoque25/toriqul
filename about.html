My Story

My path into SEO started in 2013, almost by accident. I was helping a friend with their small online business — a site that looked great but couldn't get a single visitor from Google. After weeks of digging through Search Console errors, broken redirects, and crawl logs, I made a few changes. Within two months, the site went from 0 to thousands of organic visits per month.

That moment changed everything. I realized SEO wasn't about content tricks or magic keywords — it was about how the web actually works. How Googlebot crawls. How JavaScript renders. How redirects chain. How schema parses. The deep technical layer that most agencies skip because it's hard.

So I went all-in on the technical side. I learned to read server logs, debugged crawl traps, fixed JavaScript rendering, and mapped 50-million-URL crawl architectures. Then AI search arrived — ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews started reshaping how people find businesses. I added AEO, GEO, and AI Overview optimization to my toolkit, because the same technical depth that fixes crawl issues is what makes a site AI-citation-ready.

I joined Upwork in 2014 and built my reputation one project at a time. Today I'm a Top Rated Plus freelancer with a perfect 100% Job Success Score, 220 completed projects, 5,400 hours logged, and $70k+ in total earnings on Upwork. I've worked with SaaS companies in Silicon Valley, news publishers in London, e-commerce stores across Europe, and enterprise marketplaces in the US. The platforms change, but the technical SEO principles stay consistent.

What still drives me is the moment a client sees their first ranking improvement after months of being stuck. The Search Console graph that finally goes up and to the right. The "wait, this actually worked" message after fixing something three other agencies missed. That's the work that matters — fixing what's broken so businesses can finally compete on a level playing field. That's why I show up every day.
